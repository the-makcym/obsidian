$$
e = \{\bar e_1,\ldots,\bar e_n\} - \text{система векторов}
$$
$$\Gamma =
\begin{pmatrix}
(\bar e_1, \bar e_1) & \dots &  (\bar e_1, \bar e_n)  \\
\vdots & \ddots & \vdots  \\
(\bar e_n, \bar e_1) & \dots &  (\bar e_n, \bar e_n)  \\ 
\end{pmatrix}
$$
---
$$e \text{ ЛНЗ} \iff \det(\Gamma) > 0$$
$$e \text{ ЛЗ} \iff \det(\Gamma) = 0$$
